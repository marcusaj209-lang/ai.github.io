How AI Voice Technology Works

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Text-to-Speech converts written text into spoken audio. Early systems used rules, while modern systems use AI to sound natural.

Machine Learning

Machine learning allows AI to learn patterns in human speech, including pronunciation and rhythm.

Deep Learning

Deep learning uses neural networks to analyze massive datasets of human voices, making AI speech sound realistic and expressive.

Voice Cloning

Voice cloning allows AI to replicate a specific person's voice using only a short audio sample.

Neural Networks

Neural networks process complex speech patterns and generate human-like audio waveforms.

Real-Time Voice Generation

Modern AI can generate speech instantly, allowing real-time conversations with virtual assistants and AI characters.
